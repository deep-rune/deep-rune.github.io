Gem Filters

Gem Filters are small profile four-pole filters available in either low-pass or high-pass packages. These tiny powerhouses provide classic four-pole sounds and are made with high-quality components including silver mica capacitors - GEM filters pack a huge sound in a tiny package!

Behind the Gem filter's modest appearance lurks a tonal beast. These filters can form the basis of your sound and fit conveniently in a spare 4HP rack space. The Gem is available in a low-pass package and also as the 'lesser spotted' high-pass.

Width: 4HP
Current: +20mA / -20mA @+12V / -12V
